The Invisible



"The Invisible" is referring to anyone we cannot see that ask us for our personal information online. Such as shopping sites, social media accounts, email accounts, federal websites and so on. The point is that we have become completely comfortable with putting our personal information, and basically our entire lives, on the web without thinking much of it. When, at any given moment, these sites could get hacked into or our information could be shared without our consent and the next thing you know, you are getting a million calls from places like Utah or Nebraska about timeshares! One slip on the wrong website and that's it for our privacy and security. Predators are all over the internet. Don't become a victim. Be careful with where you share your information.
